Have Questions?

Find quick answers to common inquiries about Fosslink home automation.

Where is my usage data stored? Is it sent to the cloud?

We prioritize local control for privacy. Your automation logic and personal usage data primarily run and remain stored within your home network, not on external cloud servers.

What kind of support do you offer after installation?

We provide comprehensive local support, including troubleshooting, system adjustments, usage guidance, and various ongoing support options.

Can Fosslink work completely offline without internet?

Yes, core automations, schedules, local sensor triggers, and even certain local voice commands are designed to work reliably even if your internet service is down.
